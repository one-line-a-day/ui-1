One Line a Day is the app version of the popular one-line-a-day journal. Your daily entries are privately stored on your device (mobile version), or safely online if you use our web-based version.

Our app lets you write a journal entry each day. Write a funny quote, crazy milestone, or what you ate for dinner! You can choose to set daily reminders to sign in to log your entry.

Our unique timeline allows you to review previous entries. You can search day by day or use our tools to find a specific date. Each day will present your previous entries that were written on the same day in years past.

Did you make a mistake in one of your previous entries? Our app lets you update any entry in your log!

Find an entry that makes you think, "What was I thinking when I wrote that??" Easily delete any regretful entries that you've ever made.
